Short Bio:

Abhishek Singh is a Post-Doc-Researcher in the Advanced Computing Group at the Institute for Earth Observation, Eurac Research, Bolzano, Italy, and a team member in the ScaleAgData, Podiums and Birdwatch projects, contributing to data fusion tasks for grassland monitoring in the Italian Alps and weak semantic segmentation for onboard AI. He received his Ph.D. in Information and Communication Technology with a specialisation in Space Data Science and Technology from the University of Trento, Italy, in January 2024. His research expertise encompasses weakly-supervised deep learning techniques, including generative models, convolutional neural networks and attention mechanisms, specifically applied to multi-source satellite image analysis. Additionally, his skills extend to data augmentation, data fusion, land-cover mapping and spatio-temporal data analysis.
